Who are you?

My name is Amy Louise Stevens. I'm 30 years old and I'm originally from Manchester in England, although now I live in Catalonia.

My formal education is in the field of translation and linguistics, and I worked for almost a decade in the translation sector, specialising in medical translation and literary translation. I now work as a technical writer, while continuing my work in writing and literary translation. You can view my professional website by following this link.

Why are you learning this?

I decided to learn how to use HTML, CSS and Javascript as I found there was some crossover with some of the skills I had already developed in my career as a translator and editor. I already had experience with XML, having used SDL Trados in my work.

Another reason I decided to learn is that I am always trying to learn new skills. I had reached a point in my career where I had developed my expertise and efficacy, but I felt I was no longer learning new skills. I was bored and frustrated with my job, so I felt it was the right time to try something new. I decided on web developing because I think it is very similar to learning a new language, but different enough that it poses a challenge.

How are you learning this?

I started learning through a guided project on Coursera called Introduction to HTML. I've continued to learn following the HTML, CSS, and Javascript for Wed Developers course on Coursera.
